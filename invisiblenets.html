Invisible Grill Nets

Invisible grills are a modern safety solution designed for homes and high-rise buildings. Unlike traditional window bars, these grills are made of ultra-strong stainless steel cables coated with protective PVC. They offer safety without blocking your view or compromising on design.

Why Choose Our Invisible Grills?

Our expert team ensures proper measurement, secure installation, and customized alignment to suit your specific architectural layout. Available in horizontal or vertical wiring options with tailored spacing as per your safety needs.

  • Made with 2mm 316 stainless steel for superior strength and rust resistance
  • Each wire has a PVC coating to ensure durability and safety
  • Load-bearing strength of over 450 kg, tested under pressure
  • 12+1 cable structure for greater tensile strength than regular 7x7 cables
  • Low maintenance â€“ no painting, no rust
  • Child and pet safe
  • Seamlessly blends with any building facade
